## 🍏 About Me & Computer Science Passions
I have a deep love for general Computer Science, modern web browsing, and system optimization, but my true alignment lies with digital preservation and nostalgia.

* 🍎 **Apple Legacy Experiments:** Deep diving into legacy iOS firmwares, restoring old architectures, and exploring vintage hardware limits.
* 💿 **System Installation & Education:** Learning and teaching how old and new operating systems install, boot, and behave.
* 🎮 **Retro Console Overview:** Passionate about older gaming platforms, specifically diving into the architecture and history of systems like the **Sony PS3**.
* 💻 **Hybrid Workspace:** Developing seamlessly between **Windows** and **Ubuntu 22.04 LTS (via WSL2)**.
